  • April 2018 - July 2018

    After working with the Westfarm Bridge To Tech and The Knowledge House Intro To Tech programs , Heriberto Roman an Instructor at The Knowledge House realized a pattern, a problem that existed with code newbies . The problem of how to learn new difficult technical concepts and move them into long-term memory .

  • August 2018

    Heriberto introduced the idea of an Instructor centric capstone project to the Teacher & Learning team at The Knowledge House to start building solutions for TKH built by the Instructors team. The idea was welcomed and accepted .

  • September 2018

    cThink was pitched by Heriberto Roman to the Instructor team and accepted as the first Instructor capstone project. The Project starts begins .

  • October 2018

    cThink wireframe V1 is complete
